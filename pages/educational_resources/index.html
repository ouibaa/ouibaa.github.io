Video-controlled histology tutorial application

I originally believed that if we linked a histology slide to specific time-points in a tutorial video, we would make a more immersive learning experience for students who wished to learn their histology/histopathology at home whilst also being given the freedom to explore. The idea never took off, but I still would like to revisit it.

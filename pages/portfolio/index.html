MEDS1X01 workshop

Worked with Prof. Philip Poronnik from the School of Medical Sciences and Dr. Michelle Gerke-Duncan (Anatomy and Histology) to digitise an introductory anatomy workshop for undergraduate students during the pandemic lockdown (COVID-19, 2020). This involved the digitisation of workbooks and use of anatomy museum artefacts, as well as collaboration with BioDigital to provide interactive 3D models for students.

MEDS1X01 Anatomy workshop
HTD and PNT Catchment Area

As part of my MD project, I used GIS software (qGIS) and a series of Geocoding APIs to interpret clinical data. This enabled the generation of 'catchment area' maps. As part of this research, I was able to use Google's real-time distance matrix API to estimate actual travel times from patient locales to the hospitals.

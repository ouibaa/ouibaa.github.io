MediWiki: Medicine Wiki

Link: mediwiki.github.io

A site developed from resources collected during medical school and through working in the hospital. A useful repository of information based on lectures, textbooks, experiences and resources such as UpToDate.
Only use this resource for your own education and apply at your own risk with adequate clinical reasoning and experience.
This site includes information on not just internal medicine, but surgical specialities, critical care medicine, paediatrics and psychiatry.
This site is still under development and I continue to add to it.

EpiWiki: Clinical Epidemiology Wiki

Link: epiwiki.github.io

A repository of information regarding clinical epidemiology and biostatistics. I started this resource after my Masters in Science in Medicine (Clinical Epidemiology) degree as a way of practicing and reminding myself of important skills such as research appraisal and biostatistics.
After the degree I needed much more experience with biostatistics and had to do more reading around Machine Learning, of which resources were plentiful however much too dense for someone with a medical background only. This site is thus a collection of knowledge built from other sites, lectures (including MOOCs) and my own research experiences.
This site is still under development and I continue to add to it.

STAT notes: An on-the-go medical handbook for the wards

Link: statmedicine.github.io

Born from my experiences as a new intern on the wards and not knowing how to manage sick patients, this website was developed as a resource for myself to refer to when I wasn't able to remember what to do (e.g. management of an anaphylaxis, management of hypoglycaemia and hyperglycaemia).
This component of the notes was built with the On Call textbook in mind
The other component of this site was to provide a guide for consults. I found myself poorly experienced with consults and unsure of what information was needed.
I built this component of the notes based on the JMO Handbook from Westmead Hospital.
This site is still under development and I continue to add to it.
